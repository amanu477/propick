⚡ Pro Tips (Very Important)
1. Avoid Google Penalties

If you auto-generate low-quality pages:
👉 Google will ignore or penalize your site

Fix:

Add unique insights
Add real data
Don’t mass-publish garbage
2. Focus on “Best” Pages First

Automate:

“Best VPN”
“Best Antivirus”

NOT:

Random product pages at first
3. Add Update System

Re-run automation to:

Update prices
Update ratings
Refresh rankings
4. Track Performance

Use your click tracking to:

Promote high-converting products
Remove low performers
